Mask Doner


It is a Volunteer Ran Organization to helping Medical Practionare by showing your helping hand with N95 and other Surgical Masks who are on frontline to save us from this deadly Virus attack.Over the time due to Surprise increase of requirement Medical Institute force to curb their mask distribution due to lack of supply , Many are forced to perform without any Mask, While the World is trying to produce more and serve more but we are critically unable to match the time frame for the same . We already have masks in our closets , May be you have some excess , may be you have some which you are not using and its covering with dusts in your closet , A Small Support with those could save many life’s and help those people to fight the battle with this Pandemic Situation.

How you can serve?


Everytime you are willing to donate your mask all you need to do it to click “Donate Mask” and fill the details , so that we can know about you and match with a nearest Medical Practitioner who need the same , So you can Ship your mask who needed most .


Being a Medical Practitioner all you need to perform is to click “Need Mask” and Fill the details , so we can match your neediness with the donor nearest to your Location. Our Team Verify the Identities and will inform the donor so that Donor could ship the mask and share the consignment details with us , we will verify the same and  will share the Tracking number with Healthcare worker .

*We will Verify Identities of healthcare members before making any matches , as these masks are not for purchase or  for sale, We appreciate the Donor effort to ship the same by paying their Courier Charges.
